Fireball Whiskey

BURNS LIKE HELL, Tastes Like Heaven.

Origin

Fireball was developed in Canada in the mid-1980s and was originally called Dr. McGillicuddy's Fireball Whisky.

Alcohol By Volume (ABV)

Fireball is 66 proof, or 33% ABV, which is 20% less alcohol than most whiskies.

Taste

Fireball has a smooth flavor with a subtle kick and sweet aftertaste. Some say it tastes like red hots soaked in water.
